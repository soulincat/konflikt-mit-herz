10. Photo Editing Guidelines

  1. Color grading: warm, soft, slightly muted tones; shadows soft, highlights tinted (cream/blush)
  2. Contrast: gentle, editorial; no harsh clarity
  3. Textures: optional overlays (plaster, paper, stone, fabric, fine grain), 2‚Äì10% opacity
  4. Cropping & composition: generous whitespace, vertical rhythm, asymmetric balance
  5. Typography integration: serif for meaning, light weight; soft charcoal or brown on neutral background
  6. Final touch: subtle vignette or soft gradient, avoid heavy filters or neon accents
