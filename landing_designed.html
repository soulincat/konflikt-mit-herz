Welcome to Soulful Academy

Clarity + Grounding

The Soulful Academy is a school for embodied conflict competence and conscious relationship culture.

We offer training and educational programs for individuals, groups and organizations – promoting self-efficacy, emotional clarity and connection.

Our aim is to empower people so they can express what they feel, need, and want – and stay connected with each other in the process. In both their personal and professional lives.

Clarity + Structure

The opposite of conflict is not peace, but cooperation.

Our training programs are based on an innovative and interdisciplinary approach that does not aim to resolve conflicts quickly, but rather integrates them sustainably as a tool for relationship and self-management.

It combines five levels that are effective in every conflict:

Body & Regulation
Somatic foundations and self-regulation in tension.
Emotion & Need Perception
Emotional intelligence and clarity about needs.
Consciousness & inner order
Inner clarity and decision-making.
Decision & Responsibility
Taking responsibility and clear choices.
Relationship & System
Systemic view and relationship dynamics.

It is much more than theory; it is a transformative experience.

Basic training – conflict competence with heart

The training will be accompanied live by us and will take place in a group of a maximum of 20 participants. Live support for all online and offline seminars by female founders – Isabel & Ania

What it's about

The training combines insights from psychology, neurobiology, mediation, somatic work, systemic counseling and elements of trauma work – and translates them into concrete, experiential practice. You learn:

  • to maintain tension
  • To clarify emotions
  • To take responsibility
  • to use the right tool at the right moment
  • and to stay in contact – even when it gets difficult
